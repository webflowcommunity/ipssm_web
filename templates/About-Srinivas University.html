Empowering Students for Success: Srinivas University's Commitment to Excellence

Srinivas University is a renowned institution of higher education located in Mangalore, Karnataka. It offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral programs in various fields including engineering, management, commerce, science, humanities, and more. With state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and industry-relevant curriculum, the university provides a holistic learning environment for students to achieve their academic and career goals.
